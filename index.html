Ghana Data Science Summit 2024

What happens at

INDABAX

Ghana?

The Ghana Data Science Summit (IndabaX Ghana) is the vibrant summit where Ghana's data science community converges to inspire, innovate, and illuminate the path forward in the realm of Data Science and Machine Intelligence.

Celebrating our 5th anniversary this year, we're gearing up for an unforgettable gathering in the heart of Ghana, bringing together a diverse mix of the world's most brilliant minds driving change and shaping the future of technology, analytics, and artificial intelligence.

Join us for a dynamic conference experience like no other, where you'll have the chance to network with pioneers, learn from industry leaders, and engage in capacity-building workshops designed to elevate your skills and understanding of Data Mining, Machine Learning, and AI.
