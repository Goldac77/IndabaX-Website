The Ghana Data Science Summit
Mentorship Program

This initiative is designed to bridge the gap between aspiring data science, AI, ML professionals and industry experts through personalized guidance, hands-on projects, and career development opportunities.

Benefits of the Program

  • Direct mentorship from industry leaders
  • Networking opportunities with peers and professionals
  • Hands-on projects to apply your learning in real-world scenarios
  • Enhance your resume with practical experience and projects
  • Access to exclusive workshops and webinars
